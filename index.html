Information and Accomplishments

The Red Hot Chili Peppers (RHCP) formed in Los Angeles, California in 1983. They have gone through several lineuo changes througout the years, but the lineup pictured above is their most frequent to date. Flea (bass) and Anthony Kiedid (vocals) have are the only remaining founding members of the band.

RHCP has sold over 80 million records worldwide, making them one of the most successful acts in music history. They combine different elements of hard rock, alternative rock, funk, hip hop, reggae, and jazz into their music. All of those different musical influeneces have created a truly one of a kind sound that people all around the instanly recognize.
